Tenis Oynamak

We can collect the benefits of tennis in three main groups as physical, psychological and social aspects. The most important physical benefits of tennis; it can be summarized as helping the body to burn fat in general, giving the individual agility, balance, flexibility and speed, and increasing body coordination. In addition to these physical benefits, tennis provides important contributions to the individual in a psychological sense, as it is a struggle against the opponent, partly against the wind and the sun. These benefits can be summarized as that tennis teaches the individual to struggle constantly, gives an understanding of responsibility, develops the concept of discipline, and teaches planning and strategy development in sports.
