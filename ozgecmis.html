Doğum

I am Moaz Ashraf, I am Egyptian, but I lived and grew up in Saudi Arabia. I was born in 1998 and I have 4 brothers.

İlk Eğitim

My parents are from Cairo, but they traveled to Saudi Arabia, and I was born there. I started my education at “Abi Abdul Rahman Al-Salami Primary School” and completed my education until high school in Yanbu. I was always superior, but always the first in my school and I got many awards during my trip, and that's only until I got to university and everything changed.

Yüksek Eğitim

After I finished high school, I decided to study in Turkey and because of God’s success, then my high grade got a full scholarship, and there I saw everything different for Saudi Arabia, starting from culture to the type of study, and I faced many obstacles at university, starting with the language and understanding the curriculum. But in the end it is a beautiful and enjoyable experience.

Bitirme

In the end, I consider myself a person who loves exploration and adventure, and I am going on this path and ready to try new things and explore new places always.
